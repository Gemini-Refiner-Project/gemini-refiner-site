Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Gemini Prompt Refiner. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome Extension.

2. Information Handling

The Extension is designed to refine prompts directly within the Google Gemini interface. Its core function relies on interacting with the Google Generative Language API. We handle the following information:

  • Input Prompts: The text you enter into the Gemini chat box to be refined.
  • Refined Prompts: The improved versions generated by the AI.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your privacy is our priority. This extension follows a "zero-server" philosophy:

  • Local Processing: All prompt text is handled locally within your browser session.
  • No External Storage: We do not collect, store, or transmit your prompts to our own servers.
  • Encryption: Communication with Google's APIs occurs over secure HTTPS connections.

4. Third-Party Services

By using this extension, you are also interacting with Google Gemini. Your data usage is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.
